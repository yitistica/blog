About#

Hey there! 👋

Welcome to my blog. I am Yi, an AI Engineer & data scientist based in Hong Kong & Shenzhen.

After a long stretch of working relentlessly as a consultant, I made up my mind for a change. Recently, I’ve found myself with a bit more breathing room in my schedule, I’ve decided to seize this opportunity to start blogging, consolidating my previous scattered notes, sharing ideas, industrial insights, technical tricks and even something a bit personal, if no one minds, with those who stumble upon my site.

For the past few years, I’ve been diving in the world of data science professionally, collaborating with different financial institutions to develop machine learning applications. Lately the emergence of generative AI has ignited everybody’s curiosity, including mine. I’ve been exploring the field of generative AI and its applications in the industries, especially the financial industry.

If you have further questions, or had found issues with what I wrote, like to have a chat, feel free to contact me via Linkedin or Github.
